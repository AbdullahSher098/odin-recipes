World's Best Lasagna

Description:

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling
that it's worth it! Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are
well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list
and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

prep Time: 30 mins Cook Time : 2hrs 30 mins
Additional Time: 15 mins Total Time: 3 hrs 15 mins

ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

    Step 1

  1. Gather all your ingredients.

    2. Step 2

  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch
    oven over medium heat until well browned.

    3. Step 3

  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water.
    Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt
    Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for
    about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.

    4. Step 4

  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles
    in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.

    5. Step 5

  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg
    remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.

    6. Step 6

  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

    7. Step 7

  7. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch
    baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with 1/2
    of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices.
    Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle
    with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.

    8. Step 8

  8. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
    Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray
    or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.

    9. Step 9

  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil
    and bake for an additional 25 minutes.

    10. Step 10

  10. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
